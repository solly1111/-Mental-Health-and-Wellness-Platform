Depression Self-Assessment Quiz

Answer a few questions to see if you might be experiencing symptoms of depression.

Quiz

1. How often do you feel little interest or pleasure in doing things?

2. How often do you feel down, depressed, or hopeless?

3. How often do you have trouble falling or staying asleep, or sleeping too much?

4. How often do you feel tired or have little energy?

5. How often do you experience a poor appetite or overeating?

6. How often do you feel bad about yourself, or that you are a failure or have let yourself or your family down?

7. How often do you have trouble concentrating on things, such as reading the newspaper or watching television?

8. How often do you move or speak so slowly that other people could have noticed? Or the oppositeâ€”being so fidgety or restless that you have been moving around a lot more than usual?

9. How often do you have thoughts that you would be better off dead, or of hurting yourself in some way?

10. How often do you feel that life is not worth living?

11. How often do you feel like you are unable to get going, or feel exhausted even after resting?

12. How often do you feel like you are lacking energy to complete even simple tasks?

13. How often do you feel hopeless about your future?

14. How often do you experience physical symptoms like headaches or stomach problems without any clear cause?

15. How often do you feel guilty or worthless for things that are out of your control?

16. How often do you feel like you're withdrawing from others or avoiding social activities?

17. How often do you feel like you can't stop worrying about things that might go wrong?

18. How often do you experience feelings of emptiness or loneliness?

19. How often do you experience a lack of concentration that affects your daily tasks?

20. How often do you experience feelings of hopelessness about the future?
